CWG at a glance

With over two decades of immense contribution to the information communication and technology sector, CWG Plc has continuously remained a benchmark for excellence in Africa. The comany commenced operations in Nigeria, on September 26th 1991 as Computer Warehouse Limited principally to cater for the hardware projects. Five years on, DCC Networks was established as the communication arm, to provide VSAT, Metropolitan, Wide Area and Local Are Networks to corporate organizations. In order to concentrate on demands in the area of software solutions, system and training, the Expert Edge software was acquired in 1999.

CWG Academy

The Academy was established to cater to the needs of IT professionals in the ICT industry in the locations where we operate. With over 1,500 Granduants since inception in 2010. The Academy's HQ is in Lekki, Lagos.
